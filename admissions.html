Begin Your Pulse Connect Journey Today

Joining Pulse Connect means unlocking access to AI-powered career tools, skills development programs, and a network of industry opportunities. Whether you’re a graduate preparing for your first job, an employer seeking talent, or an institution wanting to equip students for the future — our admissions process is simple and transparent.

How to Apply

1

Create Your Account

Sign up as a student, employer, or institution using our online registration form.

2

Complete Your Profile

Add relevant details such as skills, career interests, or recruitment needs to personalize your dashboard.

3

Verification & Orientation

Our team will review your information and provide an onboarding guide to help you navigate the platform.

4

Start Your Journey

Access your role-specific dashboard and begin using tools, resources, and opportunities tailored to your goals.

Admission Requirements

For Students / Graduates

Proof of enrollment or graduation from a recognized institution and a completed skills profile.

For Employers

Company registration details, recruitment needs, and a point of contact for partnership.

For Institutions

Institutional details, a designated liaison officer, and student integration plan.
