Our Story

Bridging Education and Employment

PulseTech is led by a diverse and experienced team of professionals committed to transforming how graduates transition into the workforce. Our leadership combines expertise in education, technology, and industry collaboration to ensure we deliver innovative, impactful career readiness solutions.

Digital Career Readiness Platform

Pulse Connect offers AI-powered tools such as an ATS-compliant resume builder, job application tracker, and psychometric assessments to help graduates prepare for the job market.

Skills Development & Training

The platform provides access to both online and practical training programs, from digital skills like coding to hands-on trades like welding, delivered through local training hubs.

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with employers and institutions to offer mentorship, internships, and short-term work readiness programs that give graduates real-world experience.

Data-Driven Career Matching

Using analytics and AI, Pulse Connect matches students to relevant career opportunities based on their skills, interests, and industry demand.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between education and employment by equipping graduates with digital tools, practical training, and career support to succeed in the modern job market.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s leading career readiness platform, creating a future where every graduate is confident, skilled, and employed.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We equip students with the tools and confidence to meet employer expectations and excel in their chosen fields.

Community Engagement

We work with educational institutions, industries, and graduates to create opportunities and foster mutual growth.

Innovation

We embrace emerging technologies like AI and AR/VR to deliver modern, impactful career readiness solutions.

Global Perspective

We prepare graduates to compete and collaborate in both local and international job markets.
