Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Pulse Connect collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you access our career, skills development, and networking services.

1. Introduction

By using Pulse Connect, you trust us with your personal information. We take that trust seriously and are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over your data.

This policy outlines what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage or request the deletion of your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect data to deliver a secure and personalized experience. This includes:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name and contact details
  • Login credentials
  • Profile details such as skills, career interests, or company information
  • Messages or content shared within the platform

2.2 Automatic Information

  • Device and browser type
  • Login activity and usage statistics
  • Location data if you choose to enable it

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match candidates, employers, and institutions based on profiles
  • To personalize dashboards and recommendations
  • To process job applications or event registrations
  • To maintain security and prevent fraudulent activity

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Pulse Connect is a career development and networking platform. To provide our services, keep them free or affordable, and continue improving, we may use the information we collect to generate revenue through certain business activities. We do not sell your personal data directly to third parties, but we may share limited information as described below.

4.1 Service Providers and Partners

We work with trusted partners who help us operate, provide, and improve Pulse Connect. This includes hosting providers, payment processors, analytics services, and marketing tools. These partners may process your data only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Business and Career Opportunities

When you apply for jobs, participate in events, or use our matching tools, certain information from your profile (such as skills, career interests, and location) may be shared with employers, recruiters, or institutions. This allows us to connect you with relevant opportunities and maintain the value of the platform for all members.

4.3 Advertising and Sponsored Content

We may use aggregated and anonymized data to deliver relevant advertisements or sponsored job postings. This helps keep Pulse Connect operational and supports ongoing platform improvements without compromising your personal privacy.

4.4 Legal and Compliance Requirements

We may share personal information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Prevent fraud, security breaches, or technical issues
  • Protect the safety, rights, and property of users and the company

5. Data Security

  • All communications are encrypted using SSL
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized staff only
  • We regularly review our security practices

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Control your notification and visibility settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date.
